How to get a Gmail App Password

  1. 1 Open your Google Account settings.
  2. 2 Go to Security → 2‑Step Verification.
  3. 3 At the bottom, click App passwords.
  4. 4 Generate a new password for “Mail”.
  5. 5 Paste the 16‑character code into the App password field.

Why an App Password?

  • ●Google blocks regular passwords for SMTP.
  • ●App passwords can be revoked individually without changing your account password.
  • ●Stored encrypted — never shown again after saving.
